SPACEPORT

A spaceport or cosmodrome is a site from launching
or recievin spacecraft, by analogy to a seaport for
ships or an airport for aircraft. The word spaceport,
and even more so cosmodrome, has traditionally
been used for sites capable of launching spacecraft
into orbit around Earth or on interplanetary trajectories.
